About Our School

Established in 2001, Maharana Pratap Higher Secondary School is one of the most trusted and leading educational institutions in Shivpuri, Madhya Pradesh. We are affiliated to the MP Board and offer education from Nursery to Class XII.

Our institution is committed to providing a holistic learning environment where academic excellence meets moral values. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a student-centric approach, we prepare children to become confident, responsible, and successful citizens of tomorrow.
